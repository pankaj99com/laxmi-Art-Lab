About the studio

Art that feels personal, classes that feel joyful.

Laxmi Arts Lab is a welcoming artist studio where original paintings, custom artwork, and practical painting lessons come together. The studio works with children, beginners, and hobby artists through patient, hands-on guidance, while collectors can explore available pieces, request commissions, and learn the story behind each artwork.
